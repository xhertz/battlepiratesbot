STEP 3: BACK TO FACEBOOK  

Disable Facebook's HTTPs

click to enlarge
  • Go to Account > Account Settings. 
  • Scroll down to Account Security, and uncheck the Secure Browsing (https) option.
    (our service cannot operate over HTTPS. If you are on your own home network, your security will not be compromised)

Launch Game

click to enlarge
  • With the add-on enabled (by default), launch your Facebook game as you normally would.
  • Enter your email and activation code when prompted.
